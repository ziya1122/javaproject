Marine biodiversity encompasses the vast variety of life forms and ecosystems in the oceans
Biodiversity

plastic pollution

This includes plastic waste like bottles, bags, and microplastics. These materials can harm marine life when ingested and damage habitats. Plastics do not decompose easily, persisting in oceans for hundreds of years.

Chemical Pollution

This involves harmful chemicals from agricultural runoff, industrial discharges, and oil spills. These pollutants can poison marine organisms, disrupt food chains, and cause "dead zones" where life cannot thrive.
